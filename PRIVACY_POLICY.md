# Privacy Policy

Quick Currency Converter does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

The extension stores your selected currencies and entered values locally in your browser using Chrome extension storage, so your list is remembered between popup sessions.

When you open the popup or change an amount, the extension requests current exchange rates from exchangerate.fun. If that service is unavailable, it may request rates from frankfurter.dev.

No analytics, advertising trackers, or external user accounts are used.
